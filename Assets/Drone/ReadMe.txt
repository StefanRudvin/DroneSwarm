Press "W" to fly forward.
Press "S" to fly backward.
Press "A" to move left.
Press "D" to move right.
Press "UpArrow" to fly up.
Press "DownArrow" to fly down.
Press "LeftArrow" to rotate left.
Press "RightArrow" to rotate right.

For using drone on mobile, use canvas prefab and set mobile bool in the inspector.
 
Flight, rotation and stabilization of quadcopter carried by Rigidbody.

All other explanations about working of scripts situated in the scripts.
